Online Voting System

We are developing an online voting system by talking advantage of the centralized database with a web interface. Online Voting System PHP MySQL Source Code is a web-based voting platform the place voters login to vote for their candidates relying on the organization.

The main concept of this project is build a website university of asia pacific cse department association vote, which should be able to allow student to caste their vote by online without any issue.Generally voting has to be perform by user by going to the voting center. Therefore we have to implement a online voting system by which the student can vote over the online. It is one of the greatest advantage and person who go outside the university they can vote on online by our software.

Feature

Election

This project is on “Design and development of online voting application” is to allow voters to exercise their right to express their choices regarding specific issues, recalls and/or to choose their candidate or any other representatives

Admin

. In this project we have used Bar code Reader based voter id for the purpose of voter identification or authentication which is individual and unique, it helps in maximizing the accuracy.

User

User after login he or she can view and update his or her profile and also can view candidate list and provide his or her unique bar code reader based id and he or she can doing his or her vote.

Voter

A database is created containing the id of all the voters in the constituency and of all require information as like as name, address, id, email, date of birth, gender, image etc. are stored in database.

Candidate

. User after login he or she can view and update his or her profile and also can view candidate list and provide his or her unique bar code reader based id and he or she can doing his or her vote.

Result

An admin can also view result, reset result and approve or rejected of the voter update profile request which is sent by the voter or user. Hence if this system is applied the elections would be fair, cheap and free from rigging
